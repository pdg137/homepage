This is a translation of the Werthwein family tree chart, written in German in the old cursive script, in the possession of Phyllis Ream, by Dan and Paul Grayson. Dan has gone to the local Mormon genealogical library and ordered the microfilms for Knittlingen to confirm the names and dates provided by the family tree chart; those films are made from the backup copies in the archives in Stuttgart.
